## Production notes (Variant B)

- **Color grade, cuts, music, composition, aspect:** Identical to Variant A.
- **Differentiation from A:** *only* the cold-open hook (0:00-0:08) and the intro VO (0:08-0:22) and bridge VO (0:50-1:00). Everything else is shared. This is intentional — minimum-variable bet, maximum signal.
- **Hook visual treatment:** Numbers must look weighty. Use Instrument Serif or Cormorant for the dollar figure, not a sans-serif. CrossBeam used Garamond-like serif for "permit crisis" and it carried — same instinct.
- **Hook tone:** Calm, factual, no music. The numbers do the talking. Avoid breathless or YouTube-bro ("HOLY SHIT $4 / week!?") — read them like you're reading a price tag. CrossBeam's contrarian punch worked because the *delivery* was understated. We match that energy with our metric.
